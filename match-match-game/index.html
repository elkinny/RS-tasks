Match-match game

How to play

  • The player chooses a card and turns it over.
  • Then he selects another card and turns it over.
  • If the two cards are a matching pair, for example two Templar Assassins, then he take this two cards and choose another pair card.
    • If the cards are not a match they are turned back over and choose another pair.
    • The game continues in this fashion until all the cards are played.
